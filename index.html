About Me

I'm Himaxe, a 20-year-old thumbnail designer with 3 year+ experience in thumbnail designing.
Passion for creating eye-catching, high-converting visuals that make videos stand out. I specialize in crafting thumbnails that not only grab attention but also drive engagement, using a mix of color theory, composition, and storytelling.

Whether it’s gaming, vlogs, or high-impact challenge videos, I design with both creativity and data-driven strategies to boost click-through rates. My goal is to help creators attract more viewers and grow their audience with thumbnails that tell a story.
